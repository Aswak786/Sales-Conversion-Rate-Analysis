# Sales Conversion Rate Analysis(Power BI)

# Project Overview:

This Power BI project analyzes sales conversion rates across multiple dimensions such as customer segment, product category, lead source, and region. The goal is to track performance, identify top-performing segments, and uncover areas for improvement in the sales process.

# Objectives:

1. Measure overall Sales Conversion Rate (%) and number of leads.

2. Analyze conversion by Customer Segment, Product Category, Lead Source, and Region.

3. Visualize monthly trends and year-over-year performance.

4. Provide insights to optimize marketing and sales strategies.

# Tools & Technologies:

1. Python (EDA)
   
2. Power BI Desktop

3. DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

4. Power Query

5. Excel / CSV Sales Dataset

# Key Features:

1. KPIs: Total Leads, Sales Conversion Rate (%).

2. Filters: Year, Month, Customer Segment, Product Category, Lead Source, Region.

# Visuals:

1. Donut Charts for Conversion by Category and Segment.

2. Bar Chart for Monthly Conversion Trends.

3. KPI Cards for Leads and Conversion %.

4. Regional Performance Table.

5. Interactivity: Dynamic slicers and buttons for quick time-period comparisons.

# Insights Generated:

1. Overall sales conversion rate: ~45.7% (sample month: February).

2. Retail and Enterprise segments show higher conversion efficiency.

3. Clothing and Home Appliances categories contribute most to conversions.

4. Regional analysis reveals South and West zones outperform others.

# How to Use:

1. Download the .pbix file.

2. Open it in Power BI Desktop.

3. Load or refresh your dataset.

4. Interact with filters (Year, Month, Category, etc.) to explore trends.
